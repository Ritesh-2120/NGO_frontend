Donation Management System
Includes a secure payment gateway for monetary contributions, a system for users to donate items and services with pick-up scheduling, and tools for businesses to contribute and track their social impact.
Clothing Collection and Distribution
It allows users to locate the nearest drop-off points or schedule home pick-ups, tracks and manages collected clothing through inventory management, and coordinates with local organizations to distribute clothing to those in need.
Food Redistribution System
Collects surplus or unwanted food from individuals and businesses, ensures safe handling and distribution of food items, and partners with food banks and community kitchens to distribute the food.
Educational Support
This system provides access to online courses, tutoring, and vocational training, along with information on scholarships and educational grants.
Medical Assistance
This system offers information on clinics, health camps, and medical aid, and connects users with programs providing free or subsidized medications.
Awareness Campaigns
This system provides educational materials on poverty issues and support services, and organizes and promotes local community outreach events.
Employment Opportunities
This system provides part-time roles for men in teaching, logistics, and transportation, and home-based work for women in crafting and household services.
Admin Dashboard
It manages resources such as donations, clothing, and food distributions, and provides reporting and analytics to track usage, impact, and operational metrics.
